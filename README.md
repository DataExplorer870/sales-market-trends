## 📊 Data & Methodology

<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/74038190/229223263-cf2e4b07-2615-4f87-9c38-e37600f8381a.gif" width="300" align="left">

**Data Sources:**  
10,000+ sales records • 5 markets • 100+ clients • Multiple product categories

**Analytics Framework:**
1. **Data Preparation**: Combined and cleaned data using SQL & Power Query
2. **Data Modeling**: Created star schema for easy analysis
3. **Calculations**: Used DAX for key measures
4. **Dashboards**: Built clear and interactive Power BI visuals

## 🎯 Key Findings

<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/74038190/212284115-f47cd8ff-2ffb-4b04-b5bf-4d1c14c0247f.gif" width="200" align="right">

| Area | Metric | Impact | 
|------|--------|--------|
| 🏢 **Customer Data** | Most sales came from a small number of clients | Shows dependency on few clients |
| 🌍 **Market Spread** | Delhi had the highest sales share | Shows uneven sales distribution | 
| 📦 **Product Data** | Many sales were not linked to product categories | Hard to track product trends | 
| 💰 **Profit Trends** | Some products had low or negative profit margins | Shows need for better cost tracking |

## 📈 Business Impact

<div align="center">
<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/74038190/212284175-acc7d229-8c29-4fdf-92d2-4f9fd4bbd2d3.gif" width="150">
</div>

**Short-term (Initial analysis):**
- ✅ Organized and categorized all products in dataset
- ✅ Reviewed customer base for risk patterns
- ✅ Checked market-wise sales performance
- ✅ Created automated executive-style reports

**Extended Insights (Deeper exploration):**
- 📊 Found ways to reduce dependency on few customers
- 📈 Identified opportunities to improve profit margins
- 💰 Noted patterns for more stable monthly revenue (simulated)

**Key Benefits:**
- 👁️ Clear and structured sales view
- 🎯 Easy decision-making with visuals
- ⚡ Less manual data work  
- 🚀 Better understanding of market patterns
